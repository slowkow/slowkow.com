Kamil Slowikowski is a PhD candidate in Soumya Raychaudhuri's lab at Harvard University. He is interested in applying functional genomics to understand gene regulation in human disease and drug response. His recent publication in Nature Communications used RNA-seq, flow cytometry, histology, and experimental assays to characterize a fibroblast cellular subpopulation enriched in rheumatoid arthritis joint tissue. Current work leverages RNA-seq with time series, dose response, and transcription factor knockdowns in human fibroblasts to find new regulators of inflammatory cytokine production.
