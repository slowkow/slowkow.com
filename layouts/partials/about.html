Welcome, I'm Kamil.

I'm a PhD candidate in Soumya Raychaudhuri's lab at Harvard University. As a computational biologist, my research is focused on the analysis of functional genomics data such as RNA-seq. I want to advance our understanding of how cells regulate different functions in the context of disease and drug response. I also build software tools for statistical tests and data visualization, mostly with R and Javascript.

If you'd like to learn more about my work, you can read my publications, check out my projects, or see my notes.
